steps:
  - "Heat the oven to 220°C / 200°C fan. Lightly grease a 12-hole muffin tin with [[butter]]."
  - "Roll the [[puff-pastry]] into a tight log, cut into even rounds, then press each round into the muffin tin so the pastry comes just above the rim. Chill the tray while you make the custard."
  - "Whisk [[flour]] with [[milk|quantity=60|mode=amountOnly]] of the whole milk until smooth."
  - "Warm [[milk|quantity=190]], the [[double-cream]], [[sugar]], [[cinnamon-stick]], [[lemon-peel]], [[vanilla]], and [[salt]] in a saucepan until steaming. Remove from the heat."
  - "Whisk the warm dairy into the flour paste, then whisk in [[egg-yolks]] until the custard is glossy. Strain out the cinnamon stick and lemon peel."
  - "Fill each pastry case almost to the top with the custard. Bake for 15 to 18 minutes, until the pastry is deep golden and the custard has dark blistered patches."
  - "Cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer the tarts to a rack. Serve warm, ideally with a dusting of cinnamon."
