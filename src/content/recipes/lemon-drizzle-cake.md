steps:
  - "Heat the oven to 180°C / 160°C fan. Line a loaf tin with baking paper and beat together [[butter]] and [[caster-sugar]] until pale and fluffy."
  - "Beat in [[eggs]] one at a time, then fold in [[flour]], [[baking-powder]], the zest of [[lemon-zest]], and [[milk]]."
  - "Spoon the batter into the loaf tin and bake for 45 to 55 minutes, until a skewer comes out clean."
  - "Mix [[lemon-juice|quantity=3|mode=amountOnly]] of fresh lemon juice with [[drizzle-sugar]]. Prick the hot cake all over and spoon the lemon syrup across the surface while it is still in the tin."
  - "When the cake is cool, stir [[icing-sugar]] with [[lemon-juice|quantity=2]] to make a thick glaze, then spread it over the top and leave it to set."
