steps:
  - "Wash the [[broccoli]], dry and chop into small morsels."
  - "Lightly crush the roasted pistachios using a pestle and mortar, or pulse briefly in a food processor."
  - "Finely chop the [[fresh-red-chilli]] and tear the coriander and [[mint-leaves]]. Place the [[broccoli]], pistachios, [[fresh-red-chilli]], coriander and mint in a large bowl."
  - "Pull each date in half to remove the stone, then slice each half into quarters and add to the bowl."
  - "Pour over the [[lime-chilli-dressing]] and toss everything together so the [[lime-chilli-dressing|display=dressing]] coats the ingredients."
  - "Scatter over the [[roasted-pumpkin-seeds]], squeeze over the [[lime-wedge]] and use the [[lime-wedge|display=lime wedges]] as a garnish. Season with [[salt]]."
  - "To make the [[lime-chilli-dressing|display=lime and chilli dressing]]: blitz the [[lime-chilli-dressing|display=lime and chilli dressing]] ingredients together in a mini processor or with a stick blender until completely smooth. If you have a preferred lime and chilli vinaigrette, use [[lime-chilli-dressing]] per 2 servings."
  - "Store any leftover [[lime-chilli-dressing|display=dressing]] in the fridge for 3 to 4 days; it is also great on other salads, green vegetables, avocado or grilled fish."
notes:

  - "If you prefer a milder salad, remove the seeds from the chilli before chopping."
